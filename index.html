Census Data Connector

About

Welcome to DVRPC's Census Data Connector where you can quickly access the most current Census data for your area of interest.

To get started, zoom to your area of interest. Then select the geography for which you want to explore (county, municipality, or Census tract, block group, or block for both 2010 and 2020) from the drop-down menu on the left. Click on the map to be directed to the Census Bureau's data profile page, which will open in a new tab. Here you'll find ACS and decennial Census data for the area you've selected on the map. You'll have the ability to navigate data by topic, share and export the data visualizations, view margins of error, and download the raw data.

For questions about DVRPC's Census Data Connector, please contact Kim Korejko, Manager of Data Coordination.

For more information about Census data products, please visit the Census Bureau's FAQ page.
